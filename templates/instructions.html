How to Make a Move in Chess:

  1. First, select the square of the piece you want to move.
  2. Then, select the square you want to move that piece to.
  3. Finally, press "Submit Move".

⚠️ Important: The order matters. You must first select the piece you want to move. If you select the destination square before selecting the piece, the move will not work. You may also deselect squares by clicking on them again.
